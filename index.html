B.S. Computer Science, University of Colorado Boulder

Hello, my name is John Le, a graduate from the University of Colorado Boulder with a B.S. in Computer Science. My journey in computer science began at CU Boulder, where I initially aimed for electrical engineering but shifted focus after discovering a passion for front-end development and cybersecurity through an introductory computer science course.

Throughout my academic career, I engaged in diverse projects that honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, C++, and Java. Notable projects include engineering a Mars Rover simulation with an A* Search algorithm, developing AI players for Mancala using Minimax and Alpha-Beta pruning, and redesigning a website to enhance accessibility. These experiences have strengthened my technical abilities and equipped me with strong communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills.

I am currently seeking opportunities in front-end development and software engineering, aiming to deepen my expertise and contribute to innovative projects. My goal is to excel in these fields, driving technological innovation and tackling challenging problems to enhance user experiences and cybersecurity measures.
